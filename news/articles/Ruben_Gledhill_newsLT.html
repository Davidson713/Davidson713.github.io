Candy Shop 20% off all Candy for the Weekend!


By: Ruben Gledhill

Date: 8/30/24

View PDF Version

The weekend has gotten a little sweeter! says John Marston, the owner of John's Sweets and More. There is now a 20% discount on all candy, for a limited time only, you can get anything from Tootsie Rolls to Jolly Ranchers! There is a wide selection of chocolate, gummies, and other candy. Come on down between Friday and Sunday to take advantage of the discount! Whether you're craving chocolates or candy from your childhood, the wide variety is sure to have something for everyone.


The local shop has been a sweet spot of the community, for young and older people. The people have been loyal to the store for a while, so the discount is a thank-you to the community. Customers can expect to find bins filled with every flavor imaginable, sour candies, licorice, truffles, and caramels. The shop has stocked up on its famous candy bars, made with locally sourced, and non-GMO ingredients. The shop's open hours will be extended for a chance for everyone to take advantage of the discount. It will be open from 8 AM to 9 PM. Supplies are limited, and popular items can sell out fast.


Don't Miss Out!


The major discount is expected to draw a lot of people in. With three full days to shop, and stock up on sweets, you shouldn't miss out on this weekend's special event! The store is located at 101 Main Lane, Lehighton, PA and you can order online on the website for pickup.
