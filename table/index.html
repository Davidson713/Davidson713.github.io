Properties and Facts of Different Metals

Iron, Steel, and Stainless Steel
Can It Rust? Color Additional Facts
Iron Yes White Rust is also otherwise known as iron oxide.
Steel Yes Slate-Gray Steel is an alloy of iron and carbon. (Coal and Charcoal are forms/allotropes of carbon)
Stainless Steel No Chrome-Gray Stainless steel is an alloy of steel and chromium.
  • Iron is mostly used to make steel, which is stronger and more durable.
  • Steel is used for a lot of things in our everyday lives, including for automobiles, tools, and construction beams.
  • Stainless steel is used for things like the silverware you eat with.
